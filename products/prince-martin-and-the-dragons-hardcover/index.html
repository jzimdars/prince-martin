Prince Martin and the Dragons

Can a boy prince and his pals rid the realm of devastating dragons?

Prince Martin returns with his brotherly band to oppose ancient dragons that threaten the land!

The two dragons have slept for two thousand long years. They embody the worst of humanity’s fears. They have dreamt of destruction and fire and smoke. A new cycle begins—for the dragons just woke.

And these deadly old drakes are the nastiest sort: they incinerate cities and kill just for sport. Now they’re heading out west, these two blasts from the past. Their intentions are cruel. The die has been cast.

But a quest has begun for a shield and a spear—two long-missing marvels the two dragons fear. And the Prince and his friends join this perilous quest to discover those weapons and rescue The West. They have little idea, though, what lies in store...

When kids finish this book, they’ll come begging for more. Join the Prince, and the elf, and the wolf, and Sir Ray. There’s adventure aplenty—and dragons to slay!

Ages 8 and up. Read as a stand-alone or as Book 3 in the series. Available in: Paperback, Hardcover, Kindle, and Audiobook. 9″ x 6″, 98 pages.

Is your son a reluctant reader?

The Prince Martin Epic series is a perfect fit because each book features:

  • High-stakes action-adventure and danger
  • Brilliant color illustrations that burst off the page
  • Text that appears to be prose—but subtly rhymes, gently nudging the boy along as he is encouraged to find the next rhyme

This is the perfect formula for engaging boy readers.

The Prince Martin Epic series is adventure books that fill a need: because they RHYME your son will read!

This book series will hook your son on reading because the subtly rhyming text fascinates reluctant readers, who can’t wait to discover the next epic rhyme. Your son will also love the books because of the explosive art and high-stakes action-adventure. These are not “safe” books!

Parents will appreciate these classic tales because they build character, model virtue, and inspire grit and resilience in boys. The series grows with your son: Each successive book gets longer and the plot more action-packed! And each book can be read as a stand-alone.

Introduce your son to Prince Martin today because boys need virtuous heroes!

If you have a young boy in your life, make sure he has this series!

The Prince Martin books are a rarity in our time - they unabashedly teach young boys that it is a GOOD thing to grow up to be brave, decisive, honorable men - yes, that boys can even aspire to become noble warriors! Yet, these important lessons are couched in rollicking verse that effortlessly entertains the young listeners (and introduces them to an astonishly advanced vocabulary, too). Prince Martin and the Dragon is considerably longer than the first two books, but is so compelling that it is easily read. Not to give anything away, but look for a passage that evokes Gandalf and the Balrog and is nearly as touching.
